# Privacy Policy for 诗歌 with Chords

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

诗歌 with Chords ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

诗歌 with Chords stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Hymn Content**: All hymn data (lyrics, chords, metadata) is stored locally for offline access
- **User Preferences**: Your favorite hymns and custom song lists are stored on your device
- **App Settings**: Your preferences for chord display and transpose settings

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect or transmit any personal information to our servers
- We do NOT track your usage or analytics
- We do NOT access your contacts, location, or other sensitive device data
- We do NOT use advertising or tracking technologies

## How We Use Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:
- Provide offline access to hymn content
- Remember your favorite hymns and custom lists
- Maintain your display preferences

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services

### Deep Linking

The app supports deep linking through `https://cicmusic.net` which allows you to:
- Share hymn links with others
- Open hymns directly from web links

When you click a deep link, the link is processed locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

## Internet Permissions

The app requests internet permission for:
- Deep linking functionality (opening hymns from web URLs)
- No other network activity is performed

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data security depends on your device's security settings
- We recommend using device encryption and screen lock for protection
- Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The app contains religious hymn content appropriate for all ages.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your locally stored data through the app interface
- Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
- Export your song lists (via the app's export feature, if available)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
